Kössen, Austria #

  • Launch:
    • Unterberghorn N: (47.6318,12.4318) @ 4500’ MSL topo 3D
    • Unterberghorn W: (47.6311,12.4309) @ 4500’ MSL topo 3D
    • Top W: (47.6245,12.4359) @ 5500’ MSL topo 3D
    • Top N: (47.626,12.4362) @ 5400’ MSL topo 3D
  • LZ:
    • next to lift: (47.6534,12.4226) @ 1800’ MSL topo 3D
    • Flight Bar [landing fee]: (47.6566,12.4203) @ 1800’ MSL topo 3D
  • Weather at launch:
    • Unterberghorn N: Windy
    • Unterberghorn W: Windy
    • Top W: Windy
    • Top N: Windy
  • Live weather: Weather at surrounding locations
  • Webcams: Unterberghorn, launch peak, LZ
  • Soundings:
  • WindTalker:
  • Aeronautical chart: SkyVector
  • Official page:
  • Requirements:
  • Sticker:
  • See also: ParaglidingMap PGE:22503 SpotAir Paraglidable Thermal Map
  • Notes: Lift tickets are bought at the lift. A five-ride clip card costs the same as a three-day ticket, but you can refill it for 5 EUR per ride, it is valid for many years, and multiple people can go up together on one card. Paraglider pilots ride down for free with the lift, if they don’t feel safe to fly. One trip and one-day tickets are also sold at the Flight Bar. The Flight Bar LZ has a landing fee (unless you buy their lift tickets or have their AXA insurance). Wind: The Unterberghorn webcam shows two wind socks, but look also at the trees. The meassured wind presented on the webcam is from the very top. There can be valley winds from the north and the west.
